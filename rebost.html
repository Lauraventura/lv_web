This is an editorial and illustration project for a collective that recovers traditional food preserving techniques, claiming this knowledge as a political statement.

As it is an organization with very limited fundings to edit the book, I decided everything would be in black and white, to enable an extreamly cheap manufacture as a fanzine.

↡

ILLUSTRATION

I made a set of illustrations that could be printed in Black and White without losing any readability

↡

TIPOGRAPHY

This book encourages people to copy its content and distribute it freely. In line with this philosophy, I chose to use open source fonts.
